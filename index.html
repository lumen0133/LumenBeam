System Sync Mechanics

1. Auto-Mining: Mining continues automatically for up to 5 hours while you are away.

2. Timeout: If you don't return within 5 hours, mining pauses to prevent unfair offline accumulation.

3. Final Claim: Click the SYSTEM SYNC button to collect your 5-hour offline rewards and instantly restart the mining cycle.

4. Missed Time: Any time beyond the 5-hour window will not generate tokens until you claim and restart.

How to Use Modules
Welcome to the Lumen Beam Module Workshop! Upgrade your defense grid with powerful modules that boost your credit generation.
How to Buy & Equip:
  1. Deposit TON to your connected TON wallet
  2. Connect Wallet in the Secure Vault section
  3. Browse Modules - Each module shows price in TON and mining rate
  4. Click BUY - Confirm transaction in your wallet
  5. Equip Instantly - Your grid updates and mining begins
Tip: Grid 1 always has the default module. Purchase modules to fill slots 2-10 for maximum mining power!
